Here are the applications with the extension ".elf"
RETROLauncher will search in the root of this directory and in 1 level of subfolders.

Example:
"mass:/RETROLauncher/Roms/APPS/Example.elf" - It will find it and list it.
"mass:/RETROLauncher/Roms/APPS/subfolder1/Example.elf" - It will find it and list it.
"mass:/RETROLauncher/Roms/APPS/subfolder1/subfolder2/Example.elf" - IT WILL NOT FIND IT.

Supported extensions: .elf
